About me:

I joined the Department of Applied Computing at Michigan Technological University in 2023. [Curriculum Vitae]

I obtained a Ph.D. and an M.S. in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the Binghamton University - State University of New York (SUNY), supervised by Dr. Yu Chen. I received a M.S. in Mechanical and Electrical Engineering from Nanjing University of Aeronautics & Astronautics (NUAA) in 2010 and a B.S. in Mechanical Engineering from Nanjing University of Science & Technology (NJUST) in 2007. Prior to joining Binghamton University, I have been worked in Siemens on software development and system integration from 2010 to 2016.

Research Interests:

  • Blockchain and Distributed Ledger Technology, Internet-of-Things (IoT), Machine Learning, Cloud/Fog/Edge Computing Paradigm.
  • Scalablility and Interoperability of the Decentralized Security Network Intrastructure.
  • Intelligence, Assurance and Resilience of Next-G Network (NGN).
